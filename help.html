Using Geobells

What is Geobells?

Geobells is an Android app that reminds you based on your location.

For example, let's say you want to pick up orange juice when you are near a grocery store, but you don't know exactly when you're going to be near a grocery store. With Geobells, you can create a reminer to pick up orange juice when you get near the grocery store.

How do I use Geobells?

Using Geobells is easy.

The process is similar to creating an reminder with a calendar app. You first create the reminder, specifying what you want to be reminded to do and where you want to be reminded to do it. Then, when you get near the place you specify, Geobells will send you a reminder.

Creating a reminder

When you first launch Geobells, you'll see the reminder list screen. This screen lists all the reminders that you've created. To begin creating an reminder, tap the + icon in the top right.



Reminder Title

What you want to be reminded to do.


Specific Place vs Type of Place

Choose where you want to be reminded.

To explain this, here is an example:

Let's say you want to be reminded to pick up juice when you get to a Walmart, but you only want to be reminded when you get near a specific Walmart (for example, the closest Walmart to your house). You would select "One specific place" and type the address of the specific Walmart you want to be reminded at.

However, let's say that instead of one specific store, you don't care which Walmart you get juice from. You would select "Any place" and then type the type of place, Walmart. Whenever you get near any of the 20 closest Walmarts to where you are, you'll be reminded.


Address (if you chose specific place)

The address of the place you want to be reminded at. You can search for an address using the "Search" button. You can also view or pick the address from a map with the "Pick From Map" button.


Type of Place

In this box, type the type of place you want to be reminded at. For example, if you want to be reminded when you get near a grocery store, type "grocery store". However, if you want to be reminded only when you get near Walmart, type "Walmart".

The more specific you are, the less places you will be alerted at. However, don't make this too broad; otherwise you may be reminded at places where you can't accomplish your task. For example, typing "grocery store" instead of "Walmart" would alert you more frequently, because there likely to be more grocery stores than Walmarts in your area. You will be reminded when you get near any of the 20 closest places of the type you specify.

To view the places Geobells will remind you at, press the "Show Places" button.


Description (optional)

A description to go along with the reminder


Remind when entering vs exiting

By default, Geobells will alert you when you get near a place, but in some cases you may want to be alerted when you leave a place. Then select the "Exiting" option.


Trigger when closer/farther than

Change how far or close away from the place you want to be to be reminded. Defaults to 1/2 mi.


Remind only on these days

Choose the days on which you do or don't want to be reminded.


Repeat

If enabled, the reminder will reset daily.


Toggle airplane mode/silence phone

Check these boxes if you want your phone to automatically toggle airplane mode or silence the phone when the reminder occurs.

Viewing a reminder

To view a reminder, tap its card on the home screen.

Deleting a reminder

To delete a reminder, tap its card on the home screen to view it, then press the delete button in the top right.

Setting a reminder as completed/upcoming

To set a reminder as completed or upcoming, tap its card on the home screen to view it, then press the set as upcoming or set as completed button in the top right.

This will move the reminder to the upcoming list if it is completed, and vice versa.

Being reminded

By default, when you get near the place you have specified, you will receive a notification, and a voice will read the details of the reminder to you. You can turn of the voice in the settings menu if you don't want it.

Once you've been reminded, the reminder will move from the Upcoming to the Completed section in the reminder list.

Troubleshooting

I'm not being reminded.
  1. Check to make sure location services are enabled. Open the settings app, and go to Settings > Personal > Location. Make sure the switch is set to both GPS and network location.
  2. If you picked "type of place", make sure you're still close to where you created the event. Geobells will alert you at the 20 closest locations to where you created the event. For example, if you typed "restaurant", Geobells would alert you at any of the 20 closest restaurants, but not restaurants far away from where you are.
  3. Look at the proximity setting of the event. If it's a small distance, such as 50 or 100 feet, there's a possibility that you may be passing by or not getting in range of the location. In addition, you may have gotten close to the location, but gotten out of range before Geobells had the chance to alert you. Try using a larger proximity setting and see if the problem is solved.
  4. Restart the app.
  5. Make sure your device is able to get your location. Make sure that your device has either a GPS sensor or access to a cell network (3G, 4G) with data plan. If your device doesn't have either of these, Geobells can't get your location when you're on the go and therefore can't remind you.
  6. Make sure your phone's notification volume is on. Geobells may be alerting you, but you simply may not be able to hear it.
  7. Check the enter/exit setting of the event. Make sure the enter/exit setting is what you're trying to trigger. The enter setting will remind you when you get within a distance of the place you specified, while the exit setting will remind you when you get outside of a distance of the place you specified.
  8. Restart your device.
  9. If none of these worked, this may be a problem with the app. Contact geobellstheapp@gmail.com for support.
The reminders aren't very accurate.
  1. If possible, enable GPS. GPS is significantly more accurate than network based location. Open the settings app on your device, then go to Settings > Personal > Location, and set your location to use GPS.
  2. Check for interference. In cases of heavy weather (such as thunderstorms or snow), Geobells may not be able to access the most accurate location data. In addition, if you're indoors, Geobells may not be able to access GPS or cellular network location.
  3. Turn off low power mode if it's enabled.This should improve reminder accuracy.
My phone's battery is being drained very quickly.
  1. Turn on low power mode. Low power mode makes Geobells check your location half as much, and makes it favor low power location methods like network over GPS, at the cost of accuracy. To enable low power mode, go to Settings > Other > Enable low power mode.
  2. Increase the polling interval. The polling interval is the number of seconds Geobells waits before checking your location again. A higher polling interval is better for battery life, but worse for accuracy. To change the polling interval, go to Settings > Advanced > Change polling interval and follow the instructions.
The app crashes.
  1. This is probably a bug. Restart the app. If that doesn't work, email geobellstheapp@gmail.com.
