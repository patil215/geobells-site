Geobells

Location based reminders, improved.

Never forget.

Set reminders for when you enter or exit a geographic location. Create a reminder in a couple taps using a functional, beautiful interface.

Humanly intelligent.

Set reminders for a type of place instead of just an address. Intuitively view your reminders on a map. Swipe to set a reminder as completed.

Beyond text.

Enable voice reminders and be read the details of the reminder, or switch on popup notifications to receive a clear visual heads-up. Go beyond the simple text notification.

Infinitely configurable.

Remind yourself to take out the trash when you leave the house. Bring up your shopping list at the grocery store. Or automatically silence your phone at the movie theater.
